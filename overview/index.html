Overview

From performing a varied range of workouts to interacting and working out with others, from getting suggestions on your workout accuracy to improving your workout with our gripping dynamic music, our FIT-VR application is an all-in-one fun workout package. All you will need is your Oculus Headset, controllers, and a Polar Heart Rate sensor and you will be all set for the lively workout adventure!

This project is an extension of a prior one that was developed at Paderborn University and is currently being led by the students from the Computer Science Department of Paderborn University. The previous project is now being extended to include various features such as FIT SWEAT, Full Body Tracking, Dynamic Music System, and more.
